Acer TravelMate P254

November,7 2022 10:34

This laptop was bought by my mom with RM700. It was a second hand laptop. It was ‘quiped with Intel Core i5-3230M, 4GB RAM, 500GB HDD. This laptop was used as my high-school project.

I used this laptop to do my pre-uni work. Assignments. Back then was simple. Nothing fancy because of this laptop’s simplicity. A CD ROM, one USB 3.0, 2 USB 2.0, HDMI, VGA, split headphone jack, Ethernet Jack. That is it.

After my pre-uni time, I have upgraded it to 240GB SSD. Because I scared that the hard disk of the laptop will be ded at any time soon. Right after I got the offer to enter my undergraduate university, I gave this laptop to my sister for her own personal use. I heard she always wanted a laptop. But this laptop is old. And I do feel bad after giving her old laptop. I wanted buy her a laptop too as a sign of return and also a sign of appreciation of what she did to me. Thank you sister.
