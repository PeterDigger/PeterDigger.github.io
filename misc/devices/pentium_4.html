Pentium 4

This desktop was a desktop that get me into the computer world. I remember when our family still uses the ADSL line, the speed was slow but still usable. We normally take turn to use computer and I usually use computer at around 4pm. I usually play online flash game. I remember it has MSN with the nudge thing which it shakes the whole screen.

I still remember we used to play Plants vs Zombies with that. This desktop has went through yearly reformat just to keep the system smooth performance. Now with the knowledge of maintaining the computer, our family doesnt go to shop to reformat and yet all the system are maintain by me.

The motherboard at the end got bad (unable to boot, boot loop, etc) and that’s where we decided to upgrade it to Pentium G620, a more modern system.
