Zenfone 5

This phone is one of the phone that gave me lots of memories.

I remember the first day my dad brought this phone and I disappointed him at the night. I started to play game right in front of him. Till now I hate my action. And I remember he said “早点睡啊，不要这样迟进房间。” And that time I still playing game at my room.

I remember the first time bringing this phone out to hang out with my friends and I still have the photos of them. I missed them.

This phone has made me understand more about how phone works when it comes to bootloader and custom ROMs. Now it runs the Lineage OS (Android 7.1).
