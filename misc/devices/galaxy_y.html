Samsung Galaxy Y

This phone was brought from my mom which costed about RM500 and we brought it at Saberkas. And the time we brought is around CNY of 2012. We brought it when I was form 1 in SMK DPHA Gapor. This phone was the phone that has removable battery.

It can take a really good photo. I remember bringing it out to Kuala Lumpur. I took bunch of photos with it. Love this phone man. As the time I typing this (2022), the phone has defects on the screen, buttons on the side has crumbled. I have rooted this phone and now it has modded audio eq which can power the audio equipment even more power.

The reason why I changed my phone to Zenfone 5 is the phone unable to make phone calls when I needed it to be done. Yes, it still makes phone calls but unreliably.
