Samsung Galaxy A7 2016

November,7 2022 10:48

I got this phone as a gift from my dad during 2018. This phone was a second hand phone from my eldest sister. She bought this phone as a signed-contract phone. She decided to sell this phone and get herself an iPhone 6.

This phone doesn’t need any introduction lol. 16GB internal storage and by that year’s standard, 16GB is a really small storage. Back then the only thing I like about this phone was the screen. It has an OLED screen and fast camera. I used this phone to capture all the moments in the class.

After I finished my pra-Uni, I also used it at work too. Make calls and lose weights too. The phone was sadly got destroyed by my friend. I forgive her because I know it was an accident. And now as you read, it is still with my brother-in-law. He kept the phone as I didn’t went to his house to redeem it back.
